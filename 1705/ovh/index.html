My OVH experience

Customer of 8 years

I've been using OVH's services since 2009. This is mostly on the low-end:

  • RPS (discontinued)
  • Kimsufi dedicated
  • VPS Cloud (higher end)
  • Domains
  • Web hosting
  • E-mail

Despite the many reviews of poor OVH support, I did not experience much issue.

The control panels are changing and can be confusing to use. I'd say that's the biggest downer.

The biggest up for me is their stuff just works. There were some downtimes but you always know about it quickly.

They did have multiple log-ins for different services which didn't work well for me.
